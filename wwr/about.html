Our History



Our rafting company was founded in 1995 with a mission to provide thrilling and safe river rafting adventures. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the rafting industry.

We started with just two rafts and a small team of passionate guides. Today, we have a fleet of state-of-the-art rafts and a team of experienced professionals dedicated to making your rafting experience unforgettable.
